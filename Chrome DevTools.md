## Console

### 变量打印

-   字符 - %s

```javascript
console.log('打印 %s', '1');
```

-   对象 - %o

```javascript
console.log('打印 %o', {});
```

-   数值 - %d

```javascript
console.log('打印 %s', 1);
```

-   样式 - %c

```javascript
console.log('%c 文本1', 'background: #27ae60;color:#ecf0f1;');
```
